Honor 8

Honor 8 lite now has a longer-than-ever lasting 3000mAh (*typical value) battery with an energy density of 650WHL, a power-saving 16nm chip and the latest Smart Power 5.0 system. This will give you a maximum of 93 hours of use including music playback, giving you more time for entertainment between charging. The clever little Honor 8 lite can even identify the energy status of your smartphone, and activate its multiple power-saving modes as needed, such as the ROG low-power and super-power saving modes, ensuring you can still be in touch in any low-battery situation.
